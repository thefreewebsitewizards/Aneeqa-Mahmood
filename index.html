About Me

I'm Aneeqa, a registered Counsellor dedicated to helping people feel heard, understood, and supported. I work with young adults, adults, and NDIS participants, offering a safe, non-judgemental space to explore challenges, build resilience, and create positive change.

My approach is warm, person-centred, trauma-informed, and focused on practical strategies you can use in everyday life. Whether you're navigating anxiety, stress, low self-esteem, or major life changes, we'll work together to find clarity, calm, and a path forward that feels right for you.
