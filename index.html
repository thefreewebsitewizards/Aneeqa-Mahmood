About Aneeqa Mahmood

I'm Aneeqa, a registered Counsellor dedicated to helping people feel heard, understood and supported. I work with young adults, adults and NDIS participants, offering a safe, non-judgemental space to explore challenges, build resilience and create positive change.

I provide individual counselling, Relationship counselling, NDIS Counselling and online sessions - all tailored to your needs. My approach is warm, person centered, trauma-informed and focused on practical strategies you can use in everyday life.

Whether you are navigating anxiety, stress, low self esteem or major life changes. We'll work together to find clarity, calm and path forward that feels right for you.
